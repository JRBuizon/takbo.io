Takbo Leni Kiko!

👾 Play the game!

🚀 Our Goal

  • The goal of Takbo is to reach youth groups that are possibly undecided or on the fence about their preferred candidates.
  • Through a bandwagon and testimonials approach, we hope to increase the brand and name recall of the Leni-Kiko tandem.
  • We aim to reach more groups by making the game as viral as possible within the remaining 3 weeks we have til elections.
  • You can help us do so by encouraging people to share their game experience both publicly (in their newsfeeds) and privately (in their group chats) on social media.

💗 How can you help?

  • Screenshot and post your score: Screenshot your score (as shown on the left) and post on your socials (stories), prompting users to try the game, possibly with a link to “www.takbo.io”
  • Challenge your friends: Click on the “Share” button to generate a unique link to publicly or privately challenge people to beat your score. Share on group chats, stories, newsfeeds.
  • Screen record your gameplay:  Pair this video with a prompt to get users to try the game
  • Record your reaction: Stream your authentic video reaction to playing the game on Kumu, Discord, Facebook or TikTok.
